About

Click here to view my resume in html

I was born in Madrid, Spain to Spanish and American parents. Most of my childhood was spent in a bilingual household in this city. I spent high school in an American school near Madrid, which taught the American curriculum.

The Esthetic Arts always fascinated me, and when I got to college New York Institute of Technology I saw that I enjoyed and excelled at Design. In my last year I also began to work with web design and helped my friend launch a startup called Go Big Eye, a web design firm. Here I was given the opportunity to brand the company, as well as help build their website.After graduating I was able to find a telecommuting job as an Ads Rater for Zero-Chaos; a branch of Google. I am still continuously learning new skills through Udacity and Khan Academy and similar online resources.

I have also been doing freelance websites with my brother, who is also a Web Developer. Through these odd jobs I have been able to learn a lot about Search Engine Optimization, Bootstrap, and other important elements of web design. I also learned some of the principles of game design.

As of now, I live almost exclusively off freelance work. I have yet to disappoint a client. I have a good work ethic, and always put my all into my projects. I consistently have my work done before the deadlines, and will revise it as many times as necessary. If you need to get in touch with me you can do so through my contact page.
