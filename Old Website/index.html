About

Click here to view my resume in html

I grew up between Spain and the USA, I speak both languages and I have both passports. Cultural exchanges have been a part of my life since I was born, and continue to be important in my design work. I have worked extensively in tourism, ESL and international non-profit organizations.

I have a degree in Graphic Design from NYIT, and continue to stay up to date with the latest technologies through Coursera and Udacity. I have worked in an agency environment and at several start-ups, but today most of my income comes from freelance work.

I am prolific at a broad range of tools, from the Adobe Suite (mainly Photoshop, Illustrator and InDesign) to web design (HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript and WordPress). I pick up new technologies very easily, and am able to adapt to new roles under the ever expanding umbrella of graphic design.
